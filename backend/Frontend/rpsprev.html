Intro

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic hand game played between two players. Each player simultaneously chooses one of three options—rock, paper, or scissors. The winner is determined by a simple set of rules: rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock. If both players choose the same option, the game ends in a tie.



Preview

In this quick and engaging game, each player makes their selection at the same time. The choices are then compared using predefined rules: rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. If both players pick the same option, the round results in a draw. This game is often used for fun, quick decision-making, or even as a way to settle minor disputes. Despite its simplicity, Rock Paper Scissors can involve psychological strategy, as players may try to predict their opponent's next move based on past choices.
