Intro

Flappy Bird is a simple yet addictive side-scrolling game where the player controls a bird, guiding it through gaps between vertical pipes. With a single tap to flap and stay airborne, timing and precision are key.



Preview

The bird moves forward automatically, and your only control is to tap and make it flap upwards. Avoid hitting the pipes or falling—one mistake ends the game. The minimal graphics and quick retry loop make it super engaging and frustratingly fun.
