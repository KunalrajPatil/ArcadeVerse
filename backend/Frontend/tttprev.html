Intro

Tic Tac Toe is a simple yet strategic game played on a 3x3 grid by two players. One plays as "X" and the other as "O", taking turns to mark empty squares. The goal is to align three of their symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. It's a quick and engaging way to challenge a friend or test your logic.
