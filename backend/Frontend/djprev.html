Intro

Doodle Jump is a fun, fast-paced vertical-scrolling platformer where you control a quirky four-legged creature called "The Doodler" as it jumps endlessly upward. Your goal is simple: keep jumping from platform to platform without falling.



Preview

Use left and right controls to guide the Doodler, avoid enemies, and collect power-ups like jetpacks or springs to soar higher. The game gets more challenging as you rise, with moving platforms, obstacles, and monsters. Its charming doodle-style visuals and addictive gameplay make it a classic time-killer.
